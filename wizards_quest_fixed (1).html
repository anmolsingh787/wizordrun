📖 How to Play

🎮 Basic Controls

← →Move Left / Right
SPACEJump (double jump if upgraded)
X / FCast Magic Spell
↓Enter Mushroom Houses
P / ESCPause Game
DToggle Debug Mode

🎯 Objectives

💎 Collect crystals to earn score and currency

⭐ Reach the flag at the end to complete the level

💀 Avoid enemies or stomp them from above

❓ Hit question blocks from below for rewards

🏠 Enter mushroom houses for lives, coins, or power-ups

⚡ Magic Spells

Press X or F to fire a magic bolt

Spells kill enemies at range — build combos!

Cooldown between casts (upgradeable in shop)

🔥 Combo System

Chain kills and crystal pickups without pausing

5x = 2x multiplier, 10x = 3x, 20x = 4x, 50x = 8x!

Combo timer resets if you stop collecting

🏅 Progression

📈 Earn XP from every action — level up for bonuses

💰 Spend crystals in the Shop for permanent upgrades

🎨 Unlock wizard skins to customize your character

📅 Login daily for streak bonuses (up to 200 crystals!)

🏆 Complete achievements for XP and crystal rewards

⭐ Star Rating

⭐ = Complete the level

⭐⭐ = Collect 20+ crystals

⭐⭐⭐ = 30+ crystals AND all 3 lives remaining

💡 Tips

• Buy Crystal Magnet early — auto-collects nearby gems

• Double Jump opens up many shortcuts

• Use spells to safely clear enemies from platforms

• Each world has a unique boss level (#10) — be prepared!

• Daily challenges give huge crystal rewards
